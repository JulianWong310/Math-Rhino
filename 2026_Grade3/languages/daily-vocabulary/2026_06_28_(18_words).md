**Velcro**（魔术贴、维可牢搭扣）：A fastener with two strips that stick together when pressed.

**snap**（折断、啪地打开、扣子）：To break suddenly with a loud noise, or a fastener that clicks.

**thigh**（大腿）：The part of your leg between your hip and knee.

**embroider**（刺绣、镶边、渲染）：To decorate cloth with needle and thread.

**forearm**（前臂）：The part of your arm between your elbow and wrist.

**gallon**（加仑）：A unit for measuring liquid volume.

**pint**（品脱）：A unit for measuring liquid, smaller than a quart.

**ounce**（盎司）：A unit for measuring weight or liquid.

**cloth**（布料、织物）：Material made by weaving or knitting fibers.

**wand**（魔杖、指挥棒）：A thin stick used for magic tricks or by a conductor.

**stuffing**（填充物、馅料）：Soft material used to fill pillows or toys, or food mixture inside a turkey.

**pout**（撅嘴、赌气）：To push your lips out to show you are upset.

**winder**（卷绕机、上弦钥匙）：A tool used to wind up a clock or toy.

**forecast**（预报、预测）：To predict what will happen, especially weather.

**comedy**（喜剧、滑稽、幽默）：A funny play, movie, or show.

**pull-string**（拉绳、拉线）：A cord you pull to make a toy work or sound.

**shred**（撕碎、切碎、碎条）：To tear into small pieces.

**genre**（类型、体裁）：A style or category of art, music, or writing.
