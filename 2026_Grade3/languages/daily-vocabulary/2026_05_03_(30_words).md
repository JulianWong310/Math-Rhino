**clashing**（冲突的、不协调的、撞击）：Fighting or disagreeing, or colors that do not look good together.

**symphony**（交响乐、交响曲）：A long piece of music played by an orchestra.

**performance**（表演、表现、演出）：Acting, singing, or playing music for an audience.

**flinched**（退缩、畏缩）：Moved back suddenly because of pain or fear.

**obeyed**（服从、遵守、听从）：Did what someone told you to do.

**violated**（违反、违背、侵犯）：Broke a rule or law, or treated something disrespectfully.

**slip**（滑倒、滑落、小错误、纸条）：To slide and fall, or a small mistake, or a small piece of paper.

**murmured**（低语、喃喃自语）：Spoke quietly and softly.

**stumped**（难住、困惑、树桩）：Confused because you cannot answer a question.

**fretted**（烦恼、焦虑、担心）：Worried or upset about something small.

**crib**（婴儿床、抄袭、牛栏）：A small bed for a baby, or to copy someone's work.

**babbled**（胡言乱语、牙牙学语、喋喋不休）：Talked quickly and unclearly.

**pacified**（安抚、平息、绥靖）：Calmed someone down who was angry.

**collapsed**（倒塌、崩溃、昏倒）：Fell down suddenly.

**earthquake**（地震）：A sudden shaking of the ground.

**skimmed**（撇去浮沫、略读、掠过）：Removed from the top, or read quickly, or touched lightly.

**tutor**（家庭教师、辅导老师、导师）：A private teacher who helps one student.

**nurse**（护士、哺乳、照料）：A person who takes care of sick people, or to feed a baby milk.

**puny**（弱小的、微不足道的、微弱的）：Small and weak.

**glittery**（闪闪发光的、耀眼的）：Shining with many small bright sparks.

**landmarks**（地标、里程碑、标志性建筑）：Famous or easy-to-see buildings or places.

**chattering**（喋喋不休、吱吱喳喳、颤抖声）：Talking quickly and constantly.

**passengers**（乘客、旅客）：People riding in a vehicle.

**midst**（中间、中央、当中）：The middle of something.

**despair**（绝望、失去信心）：A complete loss of hope.

**generous**（慷慨的、大方的、丰富的）：Happy to give or share.

**donation**（捐赠、捐款）：Something given to help others.

**distressed**（痛苦的、忧虑的、困苦的）：Very upset or worried.

**grieved**（悲伤、悲痛、哀悼）：Felt very sad, especially because of a death.

**bitter**（苦的、痛苦的、严寒的）：A sharp, unpleasant taste, or very cold.
