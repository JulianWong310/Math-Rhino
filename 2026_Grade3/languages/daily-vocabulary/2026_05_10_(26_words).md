**clinic**（诊所、门诊部）：A place where people go to see a doctor or nurse.

**tonsil**（扁桃体）：The small soft lumps at the back of your throat.

**stroll**（散步、闲逛）：To take a slow, relaxed walk.

**thermometer**（温度计）：An instrument that measures temperature.

**obvious**（明显的、显而易见的）：Easy to see or understand.

**naughty**（淘气的、不听话的、顽皮的）：Behaving badly or disobeying rules.

**vine**（藤、藤蔓）：A climbing or trailing plant.

**shrub**（灌木）：A small woody plant with several stems.

**teepee**（圆锥形帐篷）：A cone-shaped tent made of animal skins, used by Native Americans.

**adobe**（土坯、风干砖、泥砖）：Sun-dried brick made of clay and straw.

**hut**（小屋、棚屋）：A small, simple shelter or house.

**congruent**（全等的、一致的、相符的）：Having the same shape and size.

**unmatched**（无与伦比的、不相配的）：Not equaled by anything else, or not paired with matching item.

**route**（路线、途径）：The path you take to get somewhere.

**expression**（表达、表情、措辞）：Showing a feeling through words or face.

**disgusting**（令人恶心的、讨厌的、极糟的）：Very unpleasant or gross.

**dough**（面团、生面团）：A thick mixture of flour and water used for baking bread.

**illustrator**（插画家）：An artist who draws pictures for books.

**publisher**（出版商、发行公司）：A person or company that produces and sells books or newspapers.

**editor**（编辑、编者）：A person who prepares writing for publication.

**composer**（作曲家）：A person who writes music.

**author**（作者、作家）：A person who writes a book.

**pitch**（投掷、音高、沥青、推销、场地）：To throw, or the highness/lowness of a sound, or to try to sell something.

**wade**（涉水、蹚水、费力行走）：To walk through water or mud.

**shove**（推、猛推、硬塞）：To push something or someone roughly.

**foreign**（外国的、陌生的、异质的）：From another country.
