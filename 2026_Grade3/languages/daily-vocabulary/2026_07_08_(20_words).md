**Sherbet**（冰冻果子露、雪葩）：A frozen dessert made with fruit juice and sugar.

**embroider**（刺绣、镶边、渲染）：To decorate cloth with needle and thread.

**saddle**（马鞍、车座、背上）：A seat for riding a horse, or to put a saddle on.

**pint**（品脱）：A unit for measuring liquid, smaller than a quart.

**gallon**（加仑）：A unit for measuring liquid volume.

**harp**（竖琴）：A large stringed instrument played by plucking strings.

**wound**（伤口、创伤、缠绕）：An injury to the body, or to twist around something.

**gum**（牙龈、口香糖、树胶）：The pink flesh around your teeth, or a sticky chewing candy, or tree sap.

**Vietnamese**（越南人、越南语）：From Vietnam, or the language spoken there.

**polish**（擦亮、打磨、润色、波兰的）：To make something shiny and smooth, or from Poland.

**Arabic**（阿拉伯语、阿拉伯的）：The language of Arab countries, or relating to Arab culture.

**plywood**（胶合板）：A strong board made by gluing thin layers of wood together.

**archaic**（古老的、过时的）：Very old and no longer used.

**astray**（迷路、走失、误入歧途）：Lost or going the wrong way.

**forecast**（预报、预测）：To predict what will happen, especially weather.

**shred**（撕碎、切碎、碎条）：To tear into small pieces.

**oatmeal**（燕麦片）：Oats that are ground or rolled, eaten as a breakfast cereal.

**pout**（撅嘴、赌气）：To push your lips out to show you are upset.

**winder**（卷绕机、上弦钥匙）：A tool used to wind up a clock or toy.

**sunroof**（天窗、汽车顶窗）：A panel on a car roof that can be opened to let in light or air.
