# 🧬 Combination Challenge (Multiple Choice)



### Q1. The Soccer League
A local soccer league has **7 teams**. If every team plays every other team exactly **once**, how many games are played in total?
- **A)** 14
- **B)** 21
- **C)** 42
- **D)** 49

### Q2. The Sundae Master
Julian is picking **3 different flavors** of ice cream for a mega-sundae from a list of **5 available flavors**. How many different sundaes can he create?
- **A)** 10
- **B)** 15
- **C)** 20
- **D)** 60

### Q3. The Octagon Detective
How many different **diagonals** can be drawn inside a regular **Octagon** (an 8-sided shape)?
*(Hint: Total lines between 8 corners MINUS the 8 outside edges).*
- **A)** 20
- **B)** 28
- **C)** 40
- **D)** 56

### Q4. The Library Mission (Mirror Logic)
A class has **12 students**. The teacher needs to pick **10 students** to go to the school library. How many different groups of 10 students can be chosen?
- **A)** 12
- **B)** 66
- **C)** 120
- **D)** 132

### Q5. The Handshake Mystery (Reverse Logic)
At a party, every person shakes hands with everyone else exactly once. If there are **45 handshakes** in total, how many **people** are at the party?
- **A)** 9
- **B)** 10
- **C)** 11
- **D)** 45

### Q6. The Pizza Combo
A pizza shop offers **4 vegetable** toppings and **3 meat** toppings. Julian wants to choose exactly **2 vegetables** and **1 meat**. How many different pizzas can he create?
*(Hint: Calculate Veggie combos, then multiply by Meat choices).*
- **A)** 7
- **B)** 12
- **C)** 18
- **D)** 35

### Q7. The Circle Triangles
There are **6 points** placed on a circle. How many different **triangles** can be formed by connecting any 3 of these points?
- **A)** 18
- **B)** 20
- **C)** 40
- **D)** 120

### Q8. The Committee Selection
A school club has **10 members**. They need to choose a committee of **3 members**. However, the President **must** be one of the three. How many ways can the other 2 members be chosen?
*(Hint: Since 1 person is already picked, you are choosing 2 from the remaining 9).*
- **A)** 36
- **B)** 45
- **C)** 72
- **D)** 120

### Q9. The Path Finder
In a grid of **4 points by 2 points**, how many different lines can be drawn connecting any **2 points**?
- **A)** 8
- **B)** 16
- **C)** 28
- **D)** 56

### Q10. The Sticker Winner (Extreme Mirror)
Out of **1,000 people** at a stadium, **998 people** will win a small sticker. How many different groups of 998 winners could there be?
- **A)** 1,000
- **B)** 499,500
- **C)** 998,000
- **D)** 1,000,000

## 🗝️  Answer Key

| Q# | Ans | Logic DNA (The Shortcut) |
|:---:|:---:|:---|
| **1** | **B** | $\frac{7 \times 6}{2 \times 1} = 21$ |
| **2** | **A** | $\frac{5 \times 4 \times 3}{3 \times 2 \times 1} = 10$ |
| **3** | **A** | $\binom{8}{2} - 8 = 28 - 8 = 20$ |
| **4** | **B** | $\binom{12}{10} = \binom{12}{2} = \frac{12 \times 11}{2} = 66$ |
| **5** | **B** | $\frac{10 \times 9}{2} = 45$ (Try $n=10$) |
| **6** | **C** | $\binom{4}{2} \times 3 = 6 \times 3 = 18$ |
| **7** | **B** | $\frac{6 \times 5 \times 4}{3 \times 2 \times 1} = 20$ |
| **8** | **A** | $\binom{9}{2} = \frac{9 \times 8}{2 \times 1} = 36$ |
| **9** | **C** | $\binom{8}{2} = \frac{8 \times 7}{2 \times 1} = 28$ |
| **10**| **B** | $\binom{1000}{998} = \binom{1000}{2} = \frac{1000 \times 999}{2} = 500 \times 999 = 499,500$ |
