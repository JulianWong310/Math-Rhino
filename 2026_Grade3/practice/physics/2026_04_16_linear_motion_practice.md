## 2. Work Problems (Show your steps)

### Problem 1: The Morning Walk
A person walks along a straight path for morning exercise. He covers a distance of **5.4 km** in **1 hour and 12 minutes**.
* **Q1:** Calculate his average speed in **km/h**.
* **Q2:** What is his speed in **m/s**?

### Problem 2: The School Bus
A school bus travels from the first bus stop to the sixth bus stop along a straight road. The distance between each adjacent bus stop is exactly **400 meters**. If the bus takes **10 minutes** to travel from the first stop to the sixth stop (including waiting time):
* **Q:** What is the average speed of the bus in **m/s**?

### Problem 3: The High-Speed Train (HSR)
Referencing the Beijing-Shanghai HSR timetable:
* The train leaves **Jinan West** at **13:35** and arrives at **Nanjing South** at **15:33**.
* The distance between these two stations is **617 km** (1023 km - 406 km).
* **Q:** Calculate the average speed of the train for this specific segment in **km/h**.

### Problem 4: The Echo Experiment
Julian stands in front of a large valley and shouts. He hears his echo bouncing back from the opposite cliff after **2.5 seconds**. 
* If the speed of sound in air is **340 m/s**, how wide is the valley (the distance to the cliff)?
