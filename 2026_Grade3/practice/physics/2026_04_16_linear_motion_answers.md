## 3. Answer Key (Detailed Solutions)

### Unit Conversions:
1.  **Cheetah:** $30 \times 3.6 = \mathbf{108 \text{ km/h}}$
2.  **Car:** $108 / 3.6 = \mathbf{30 \text{ m/s}}$

### Problem 1 (The Walker):
* **Step 1:** Convert time to hours. $1 \text{ h } 12 \text{ min} = 1 + (12/60) = 1.2 \text{ hours}$.
* **Step 2 (km/h):** $v = 5.4 \text{ km} / 1.2 \text{ h} = \mathbf{4.5 \text{ km/h}}$.
* **Step 3 (m/s):** $4.5 / 3.6 = \mathbf{1.25 \text{ m/s}}$.

### Problem 2 (The Bus):
* **Step 1:** Calculate total distance. From the 1st to the 6th stop, there are **5 intervals**. 
    * Total Distance $s = 5 \times 400 \text{ m} = 2000 \text{ m}$.
* **Step 2:** Convert time to seconds. $10 \text{ min} = 600 \text{ s}$.
* **Step 3:** $v = 2000 \text{ m} / 600 \text{ s} \approx \mathbf{3.33 \text{ m/s}}$.

### Problem 3 (The Train):
* **Step 1:** Calculate travel time. From 13:35 to 15:33 is **1 hour and 58 minutes**.
    * Time in hours = $1 + (58/60) \approx 1.967 \text{ hours}$.
* **Step 2:** $v = 617 \text{ km} / 1.967 \text{ h} \approx \mathbf{313.7 \text{ km/h}}$.

### Problem 4 (The Echo):
* **Step 1:** Total distance sound traveled (round trip) = $340 \text{ m/s} \times 2.5 \text{ s} = 850 \text{ m}$.
* **Step 2:** Distance to cliff (one way) = $850 \text{ m} / 2 = \mathbf{425 \text{ m}}$.
