# 🎓Three Ways to Represent a Function

---

## 1. The Table Method (列表法)
This method uses a **Table (表格)** to list the inputs ($x$) and their matching outputs ($y$) directly.

* **Pros:** It’s very clear! You can see the exact values at a glance.
* **Cons:** It only shows a few points, not the whole story.

| $x$ (Input) | 1 | 2 | 3 | 4 | 5 |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| $y$ (Output) | 2 | 4 | 6 | 8 | 10 |

> **Key Word:** **Table Method (列表法)**

---

## 2. The Graphical Method (图象法)
We draw the relationship on a **Coordinate Plane (坐标系)**. Each pair of $(x, y)$ becomes a point on the graph.

* **Pros:** You can see the "trend" (is it going up? is it a curve?).
* **Cons:** It’s hard to get an exact number just by looking at the picture.

[Image of a linear function graph on a coordinate plane]

> **Key Word:** **Graphical Method (图象法)**

---

## 3. The Algebraic Method / Formula Method (解析法)
This is the most powerful way! We use an **Equation (等式)** or **Formula (公式)** to describe the rule.

* **Pros:** You can calculate the output for *any* input, even huge numbers.
* **Example:** $y = 2x + 1$
    * If $x = 100$, then $y = 2(100) + 1 = 201$.

> **Key Word:** **Algebraic Method / Formula Method (解析法)**

---

## 🌟 Summary: Which one to use?
* Want to see exact values quickly? Use the **Table**.
* Want to see the shape and movement? Use the **Graph**.
* Want to do complex calculations? Use the **Formula**.

# 📝 Practice Problems 

### Part 1: Basic Concept Review 

**Problem 1**
Which of the following describes $y$ as a **Function** of $x$?
* (A) $y^2 = x$
* (B) $|y| = x$
* (C) $y = x^2$
* (D) $y = \pm \sqrt{x}$

**Problem 2**
In the formula $y = \frac{3}{2x-1}$, what is the **Independent Variable (自变量)** and its **Domain (定义域)**?

---

### Part 2: Evaluating Functions

**Problem 3**
Given the function $f(x) = \frac{1}{x+1}$:
1. Find $f(0)$.
2. Find $f(2)$.
3. Find $f(-2)$.

**Problem 4**
Given $f(x) = 2x - 3$:
1. If $x = 5$, what is $f(5)$?
2. If $f(a) = 7$, what is the value of $a$?

---

### Part 3: Finding the Domain 

**Problem 5**
Find the **Domain** for the following functions:
1. $y = \frac{x-1}{x+2}$
2. $y = \sqrt{3x - 6}$
3. $y = \frac{\sqrt{x-1}}{x-3}$
4. $y = (x-5)^0$

---

### Part 4: Practical Applications 

**Problem 6: The Filling Pool**
A swimming pool currently has $100 \text{ m}^3$ of water. A pump adds water at a rate of $20 \text{ m}^3$ per hour.
1. Write the function $V$ (Total Volume) in terms of $t$ (Time in hours).
2. If the pool's max capacity is $300 \text{ m}^3$, what is the **Domain (定义域)** of $t$?

**Problem 7: Triangle Geometry**
In a triangle with a fixed base of $6 \text{ cm}$, the area $S$ changes as the height $h$ changes.
1. Write the formula for $S$ in terms of $h$.
2. Is $S$ a function of $h$?
3. When $h = 5 \text{ cm}$, what is the Area?
