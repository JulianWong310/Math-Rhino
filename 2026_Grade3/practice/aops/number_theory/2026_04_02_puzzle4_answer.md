## 🧬 Mission 1: The "Prime DNA" Decryption
* **Level 1 (Divisible by 45?):**  $12 = 2^2 \cdot 3$
    * $15 = 3 \cdot 5$
    * $14 = 2 \cdot 7$
    * **Total DNA:** $2^3 \cdot 3^2 \cdot 5 \cdot 7$. 
    * The DNA of **45** is $3^2 \cdot 5$. Since the total DNA contains all the "genes" of 45:
    * **Ans: YES**
* **Level 2 (Trailing Zeros):** Zeros are formed by pairs of $2 \cdot 5$. In numbers 1–10, we have a "5" from the number 5 and another "5" from the number 10. 
    * **Ans: 2 zeros**
* **Level 3 (Lucky Perfect Square):** * $24 = 2^3 \cdot 3^1$. 
    * To be a perfect square, all exponents must be even. We need one more "2" and one more "3". 
    * $2 \cdot 3 = 6$. 
    * **Ans: 6**
* **Level 4 (Divisor Hack):** $72 = 2^3 \cdot 3^2$. 
    * **Formula:** $(3+1) \cdot (2+1) = 4 \cdot 3 = 12$. 
    * **Ans: 12 divisors**
