# 🗝️ Answer Key for Mom

| Level | Answer | Quick Logic for Mom |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **1** | **89** | 97 is an Emirp (79 is prime). **89 is prime**, but 98 is even. So 89 is NOT an emirp. |
| **2** | **$N+2$** | $(N+2)! = (N+2)(N+1)N!$. The $(N+1)$ and $N!$ both cancel out! |
| **3** | **$n = 4$** | $n!(n+1) = (n+1)!$. If $(n+1)! = 120$, then $n+1=5$, so $n=4$. |
| **4** | **144** | $6! = 720$. Prime pairs are $(2^2) \times (2^2) \times (3^2) = 16 \times 9 = 144$. |
| **5** | **145** | $1! + 4! + 5! = 1 + 24 + 120 = 145$. (This is the most famous one!) |
| **6** | **300,000** | $3 \text{ letters} \times 10 \times 10 \times 10 \times 10 \times 10 = 300,000$. |
