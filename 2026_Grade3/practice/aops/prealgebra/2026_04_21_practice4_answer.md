### Part I: Decimal Multiplication and Division
1. $0.6 \times 0.07 = \mathbf{0.042}$
2. $1.5 \times 0.4 = \mathbf{0.6}$
3. $0.08 \times 0.12 = \mathbf{0.0096}$
4. $5.6 \div 0.08 = \mathbf{70}$
5. $0.72 \div 1.2 = \mathbf{0.6}$

### Part II: Fraction Addition, Subtraction, Multiplication, and Division
1. $\frac{3}{10} + \frac{4}{15} = \frac{9+8}{30} = \mathbf{\frac{17}{30}}$
2. $2\frac{1}{2} - \frac{7}{8} = \frac{20}{8} - \frac{7}{8} = \mathbf{1\frac{5}{8}}$ (or $\mathbf{\frac{13}{8}}$)
3. $\frac{8}{21} \times \frac{7}{12} = \frac{2 \times 1}{3 \times 3} = \mathbf{\frac{2}{9}}$
4. $\frac{5}{9} \div \frac{10}{27} = \frac{5}{9} \times \frac{27}{10} = \mathbf{\frac{3}{2}}$
5. $(\frac{2}{3} \times \frac{9}{10}) \div \frac{3}{5} = \frac{3}{5} \div \frac{3}{5} = \mathbf{1}$

---

### Part III: Conversion Between Decimals and Fractions
1. $0.875 = \mathbf{\frac{7}{8}}$
2. $0.16 = \frac{16}{100} = \mathbf{\frac{4}{25}}$
3. $\frac{9}{20} = \mathbf{0.45}$
4. $2\frac{1}{4} = \mathbf{2.25}$
5. $\frac{13}{50} = \mathbf{0.26}$

---

### Part IV: Mixed Integer Operations (with Parentheses)
1. $(45 + 55) \div (25 - 20) = 100 \div 5 = \mathbf{20}$
2. $12 \times [ (30 + 20) \div 10 ] = 12 \times 5 = \mathbf{60}$
3. $[ 160 - (40 \times 2) ] \div 8 = 80 \div 8 = \mathbf{10}$
4. $(120 \div 3) + (15 \times 4) = 40 + 60 = \mathbf{100}$
5. $200 \div [ 5 \times (12 - 4) ] = 200 \div 40 = \mathbf{5}$

---

### Part V: Powers and Exponents (Integer Results)
1. $2^3 \times 2^2 = 2^5 = \mathbf{32}$
2. $(3^2)^2 = 3^4 = \mathbf{81}$
3. $10^3 \div 10^1 = 10^2 = \mathbf{100}$
4. $(2^2)^3 = 2^6 = \mathbf{64}$
5. $4^3 \times 2^0 = 64 \times 1 = \mathbf{64}$

---

### Part VI: Permutations and Combinations
1. $C_7^2 = \frac{7 \times 6}{2 \times 1} = \mathbf{21}$
2. $C_{10}^2 = \frac{10 \times 9}{2 \times 1} = \mathbf{45}$
3. $A_6^2 = 6 \times 5 = \mathbf{30}$
4. $A_3^3 = 3 \times 2 \times 1 = \mathbf{6}$
5. $C_4^0 + C_4^4 = 1 + 1 = \mathbf{2}$
