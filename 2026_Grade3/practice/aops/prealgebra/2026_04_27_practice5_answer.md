#### Part 4: The "Sign-Flip" Challenge (Expanded Lines Only)

**Problem 1:**  
$(7b - 4) - (-3b + 6) = 15 - 5$  
→ $7b - 4 + 3b - 6 = 10$

**Problem 2:**  
$(10x - 8) - (4x - 12) = 30 - 10$  
→ $10x - 8 - 4x + 12 = 20$

**Problem 3:**  
First expand (Eq 1): $5y + 10 = 25$  
Then subtract: $(5y + 10) - (2y - 7) = 25 - 10$  
→ $5y + 10 - 2y + 7 = 15$
