**postcard**（明信片）：A small card sent by mail with a picture on one side.

**excuse**（借口、原谅、宽恕）：A reason given to explain a mistake, or to forgive.

**autograph**（亲笔签名）：A person's signature, especially a famous person's.

**fingerprint**（指纹、手印）：The unique pattern of lines on a fingertip.

**caution**（小心、谨慎、警告）：Care to avoid danger, or a warning.

**desire**（渴望、愿望、欲望）：A strong feeling of wanting something.

**cot**（婴儿床、折叠床、吊床）：A small bed for a baby, or a simple foldable bed.

**cowboy**（牛仔）：A person who herds cattle on a horse.

**daisy**（雏菊）：A small white flower with a yellow center.

**metronome**（节拍器）：A device that ticks at a steady pace to help musicians keep time.

**tweezers**（镊子、小钳子）：A small tool for picking up tiny objects.

**fibers**（纤维）：Thin, thread-like strands that make up plants, fabrics, or foods.

**waded**（涉水、蹚水）：Walked through water or mud.

**promising**（有希望的、有前途的、大有可为的）：Likely to be good or successful in the future.

**deflate**（放气、缩小、泄气、使沮丧）：To let air out of something, or to lose confidence.
