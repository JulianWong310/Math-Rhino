**aisle**（过道、通道）：A walkway between rows of seats or shelves.

**relatives**（亲戚、亲属）：Family members.

**carat**（克拉）：A unit for measuring the weight of gemstones like diamonds.

**enormous**（巨大的、庞大的）：Very, very large.

**scent**（气味、香味、香水）：A pleasant or noticeable smell.

**doze**（打盹、小睡）：To sleep lightly for a short time.

**valley**（山谷、溪谷）：Low land between hills or mountains.

**maid**（女仆、侍女、少女）：A female servant who cleans houses.

**seaweed**（海草、海藻）：Plants that grow in the ocean.

**sculpture**（雕塑、雕像）：A work of art made by carving or shaping stone, clay, or wood.

**snout**（口鼻部、长鼻子）：The long nose and mouth of an animal.

**pinecones**（松果、松塔）：The cone-shaped fruit of a pine tree.

**shrubs**（灌木）：Small woody plants with several stems.

**eskimos**（爱斯基摩人、因纽特人）：A group of people who live in the Arctic.

**colonial**（殖民的、殖民地时期）：Relating to a colony or the time when colonies were established.

**Indian**（印第安人、印度的）：A person from India, or a Native American.

**cavity**（洞、腔、蛀牙）：A hollow space, or a hole in a tooth.

**braces**（牙套、支架、大括号）：A device that straightens teeth.

**wrench**（扳手、扭伤、猛拉）：A tool for turning nuts and bolts.

**lull**（使安静、哄睡、平静期）：To calm someone or make them sleepy, or a quiet period.

**dread**（恐惧、担忧、害怕）：A strong fear of something bad that might happen.

**hammock**（吊床）：A bed made of cloth or netting hung between two trees or poles.
