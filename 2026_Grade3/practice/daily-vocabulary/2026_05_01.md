**glossy**（有光泽的、光滑的、光亮的）：Shiny and smooth.

**sympathy**（同情、同情心、慰问）：Understanding and caring about someone's pain.

**humorous**（幽默的、滑稽的、有趣的）：Funny and amusing.

**manage**（管理、应付、设法做到）：To be in charge of something, or to cope with difficulty.

**blur**（模糊、弄脏、使模糊不清）：Something you cannot see clearly.

**wither**（枯萎、凋谢、衰弱）：To dry up and die.

**flaw**（缺点、瑕疵、缺陷）：A small mistake or imperfection.

**bloom**（开花、绽放、变得健康）：To produce flowers, or to look healthy.

**approaching**（靠近、接近、临近）：Coming nearer to someone or something.

**dank**（阴湿的、潮湿发霉的）：Unpleasantly damp and cold.

**gloomy**（昏暗的、忧郁的、沮丧的）：Dark or sad.

**measure**（测量、估量、尺寸、措施）：To find the size or amount of something.

**deny**（否认、拒绝承认、拒绝给予）：To say something is not true, or to refuse to give.

**refuse**（拒绝、拒绝接受、垃圾）：To say no, or trash/waste.

**custodian**（管理员、保管人、监护人）：A person who takes care of a building.

**wires**（电线、金属丝）：Thin, flexible metal threads that carry electricity.

**picnic**（野餐、野餐活动）：An outdoor meal eaten in a park or countryside.

**club**（俱乐部、棍棒、球杆）：A group of people who meet for an activity, or a heavy stick.

**state**（州、状态、陈述）：A region of a country, or condition, or to say something.

**duty**（责任、职责、义务、税）：Something you are required to do.

**starving**（饥饿的、饿得要死的）：Very, very hungry.

**fed**（喂养、喂食）：Gave food to someone or an animal.

**romp**（嬉戏打闹、蹦跳、轻松取胜）：To play in a lively, happy way.

**substitute**（替代品、替补、代替）：A person or thing that takes the place of another.

**stain**（污渍、弄脏、染色）：A dirty mark that is hard to remove.

**embroider**（刺绣、镶边、渲染）：To decorate cloth with needle and thread.

**mend**（修补、修理、愈合）：To fix something broken.

**soda**（苏打水、汽水、碳酸钠）：A fizzy, sweet drink, or baking soda.

**pollute**（污染、弄脏、玷污）：To make air, water, or land dirty and harmful.

**theatre**（剧院、电影院、戏剧）：A building where plays or movies are shown.

**hotel**（酒店、旅馆）：A building where travelers pay to sleep.

**pondered**（仔细思考、沉思）：Thought about something carefully.

**praised**（赞扬、称赞、赞美）：Said good things about someone.

**attached**（附上的、连接的、依恋的）：Fastened or joined to something, or feeling fond of.

**schedule**（时间表、日程安排、计划）：A plan that shows when things will happen.
